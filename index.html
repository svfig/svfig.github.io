SVFIG meets on the fourth Saturday of each month with the following exceptions:

Meeting Schedule
=========

10:15 --- Sound Check
     We'll be trying out a few things in preperation for Forth Day.  The plan is to connect the audio from the wireless mike to the room PA, as well as maintaining the connection to the live YouTube stream.
      We'll also be trying out the handheld wireless mikes David Jaffe bought for us.  Does anyone have a line-level mixer?  We may decide to mix these in for Q&A or random heckelers.

=========

10:44 --- Mailing List --- George Perry
       We've had some issues with Zork lately.  George will tell us what's going on and we may discuss moving on to a different mailing list instrumentality.

=========

11:04 --- Coming Attractions: Forth Day --- Kevin Appert
        Kevin will run through the speakers who have put their hats in the ring for Forth Day.

=========

11:30 --- Lunch
We'll have lunch at the Treehouse.
http://treehousestanford.com/

13:00 --- Introductions, Gossip, New Things

     * And more!
    
==========

13:45 --- Break

16:00 --- Clean Up and Adjourn
