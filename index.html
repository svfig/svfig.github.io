* ALL-DAY FPGA eP8080 WORKSHOP   May 28
* RENDEZVOUS AT MAKER FAIRE   May 20, 21, & 22

A DIY Processor Workshop
We can do a DIY CPU workshop in May.  Participants will buy themselves a Brevia2 FPGA Kit from Lattice Semiconductors for about $50.  Ting will distribute an eP8080 package with which you can practice synthesizing a CPU on Brevia2.  Then we can talk about and do experiments on old and new CPU architectures, and emulate them on Brevia2.
