Meeting Schedule
=========

10:30 --- Quantum Computing --- Jack J. Woehr
     Jax will present his annual review of this exciting field.

=========

12:00 --- Lunch
We'll have lunch at the CoHo (Stanford Coffee House)
Treehouse is not open.
http://treehousestanford.com/

==========

13:00 ---  Forth for WiFi Chip ESP8266 --- C.H. Ting
   "I got eForth to run on ESP8266 WiFi chip. I was able to run it through UDP packets. I am still learning WiFi tricks. I hope to show that I can compile and execute new words on ESP8266 remotely."

==========

14:30 --- Break

==========

14:50 --- How to Build Primitives in Creole Forth for Excel --- Joseph O'Connor
     In Creole Forth for Excel (CFE) extensions to the language are commonly added as primitives written in VBA (analogous to writing CODE words in other Forths). This tutorial will show how to get started writing your own primitives within a few minutes.

==========

15:30 --- Ad Hoc, Ad Lib, Random Blather
We'll talk about stuff, discuss Forth Day, maybe show some videos.

==========

16:00 --- Clean Up and Adjourn
