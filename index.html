10:30 --- Bach's B Minor Mass and Michelangelo's Fresco Paintings --- C.H.Ting
     "I am revising my B-Minor Mass/Sistine Chapel Fresco video.  Spending so much time on Michelangelo's fresco painting, I had arrived at a theory that Michelangelo's design is of a hyper space architecture, originally designed for the tomb of Pope Julius II.  Pope pulled him from the tomb project to paint Sistine Chapel,  He planned to carve 40 statues for the tomb, but never had the chance to start it.  Instead, he painted the statues on the ceiling of Sistine Chapel.  He painted the outside views of the tomb inside the Chapel.  That's what I called hyper space architecture.
     Ting will explain Mishelangelo's hyper space design, and share a few clips of the music video with us.  It should be very entertaining.
